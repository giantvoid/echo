Why Echo

Built for focus, not friction.

Everything you need to think and write. Nothing you don't.

Blazing fast

Rust-powered backend. Opens instantly, searches in milliseconds, never gets sluggish.

Plain Markdown files

Your notes are just `.md` files on disk. No databases, no lock-in, no surprises.

Daily notes

Use the built-in calendar to navigate or create daily notes with a single click - or just type a date path and Echo does the rest.

Instant fuzzy search

Find any note as you type. Fuzzy matching across titles and content - no waiting.

Beautiful focus mode

Strip away all distractions. Just you, your words, and a calm full-screen canvas.

Multiple themes

Choose from a curated set of dark and light themes that are easy on the eyes.

Keyboard first

Navigate, search, and write without lifting your hands from the keys - shortcuts for everything that matters, none that don't.

Free and open source

MIT licensed. No subscriptions, no telemetry, no strings attached.
